Background


Early Life and Education:
I was born and raised in the picturesque hills of Nepal, a country celebrated for its stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage. Growing up in such an environment, technology was not initially a focal point in my life. However, as I matured, I developed a keen interest in the transformative power of technology and its potential to shape the future. This realization led me to pursue a deeper understanding of the field.

Academic Journey:
My academic journey began at Everest College in Nepal, where I was fortunate to receive a full scholarship. This opportunity provided me with a solid educational foundation and ignited my passion for learning. The experiences and knowledge gained at Everest College were instrumental in preparing me for my subsequent studies.

Eager to expand my horizons, I moved on to LaGuardia Community College in New York to study Computer Science. At LaGuardia, I dedicated myself to my coursework and projects, graduating with honors and a GPA of 3.89. The rigorous academic environment and hands-on experience at LaGuardia significantly enhanced my understanding of technology.

Following my graduation from LaGuardia, I transferred to York College to further my studies. My time at York College has been both challenging and rewarding. I have had the opportunity to delve into advanced topics in Computer Science and engage in meaningful research. I am honored to have been awarded an NSF scholarship, which has provided crucial support for my studies and research endeavors.

Future Aspirations:
Looking ahead, I am enthusiastic about the possibilities in the tech industry. One of my primary goals is to develop a soccer game that combines my passion for the sport with my growing expertise in technology. I am eager to create innovative and immersive digital experiences that can connect people and enhance their enjoyment of the game.

Personal Interests:
Outside of academics, I have a diverse set of interests that keep me engaged and motivated. I am passionate about soccer and enjoy following and playing the sport whenever possible. Chess is another favorite pastime, as it challenges my strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. Additionally, I stay informed by keeping up with current events and news, which helps me remain connected with the world and informed about global developments.
