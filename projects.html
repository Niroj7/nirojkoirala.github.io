Problems in Mega-cities

This project focuses on the challenges faced by mega-cities, urban centers with populations exceeding 10 million. The research delves into critical issues such as inadequate housing, strained infrastructure, traffic congestion, and environmental degradation. The study proposes solutions like sustainable urban planning, green infrastructure, and community-centered approaches to improve living standards while reducing inequalities and pollution in these rapidly growing cities.

AI Impact on Jobs

This research examines how Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the workforce, automating repetitive tasks, and enabling new opportunities in emerging fields. It discusses the industries most affected by AI-driven changes, such as manufacturing and logistics, while emphasizing the importance of reskilling workers. The study advocates for policies promoting human-AI collaboration, adaptive education systems, and investments in innovation to mitigate the disruptive effects of automation.

Research on Electromagnetic Waves

This project investigates the effects of electromagnetic waves emitted by devices like Wi-Fi routers, mobile phones, and microwave ovens. It explores their potential health risks, including long-term exposure concerns, and presents findings that highlight the importance of public awareness. The study advocates for updated safety regulations and further research into electromagnetic wave frequencies and their biological impact to ensure safe and sustainable technology usage.

Smart Traffic Light Control System

This project designs and implements a smart traffic light control system using Arduino microcontrollers to enhance traffic flow and road safety. The system integrates real-time vehicle density detection through ultrasonic sensors, prioritizes emergency vehicles using sound sensors, and includes manual overrides via Bluetooth for flexibility. The inclusion of pedestrian-friendly crosswalks and boom barriers adds an extra layer of safety. The solution is cost-effective and scalable, offering significant benefits for small to medium-sized urban areas.
